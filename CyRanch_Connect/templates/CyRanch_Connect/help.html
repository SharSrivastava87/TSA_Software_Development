Help

Welcome to the interactive help menu for Cypress Connect, click on the section you would like to learn more about below:

Cypress Connect is an application to encourage students to get involved in school activities and earn prizes while doing so!

Simply login using your student ID and password!

Earn Points by attending events and use them to redeem prizes! Each event will reward a different amount of points as shown on the events tab. You can also see where you rank in your grade level on the leaderboard!

Win prizes by collecting points! Each quarter one student from each grade is selected at random to win a prize along with the student with the most points overall. You can view the prizes offered for this quarter on the prizes tab.

Events can be seen on the events tab. Each event will reward a different amount of points. These points will be added to your account when you attend or participate in an event.
